Welcome to the
5th Edition

TECHTONIK 5.0 is the flagship inter-school IT fest that has ignited the minds of young innovators for five extraordinary years — a vibrant platform where creativity meets code, and ideas meet their moment.

This edition draws from the ancient Japanese art of Kintsugi — where broken pottery is mended with gold, becoming more beautiful for having been broken. Applied digitally: technology doesn't just fix broken systems, it transforms them into something stronger, rarer, and more magnificent.

Centred on AI, IoT, and real-world problem solving, TECHTONIK 5.0 invites students to rebuild with purpose — creating solutions that are sustainable, scalable, and deeply impactful.

"The wound is the place where the light enters you." — Rumi
The crack is where the gold pours in.

◆   Rules   ◆

The Code of Conduct

Every innovator must know the rules of the arena. These laws ensure a fair, original, and extraordinary competition.

RULE 01
⚔️
Each student may participate in one event only across all categories. Choose your battlefield wisely.
RULE 02
🏆
The overall winner is determined by the cumulative total of school points earned across all events.
RULE 03
⚖️
All judges' decisions are final and irrevocable. No appeals shall be entertained after declaration.
RULE 04
📁
A strict submission naming format is mandatory. Incorrectly named files result in automatic disqualification.
RULE 05
🔒
No school names or identifying information may appear inside submitted files or presentations.
RULE 06
✍️
Original work only. Any plagiarism leads to immediate and permanent disqualification.

◆   Judging   ◆

The Six Pillars

Every submission is measured across six gold standards of innovation, impact, and brilliance.

✦
Creativity
Originality of concept and boldness of execution. Does it stand apart from everything else?
⚡
Innovation
Novel, boundary-pushing use of technology. Does it reimagine what's possible?
◎
Clarity
How crisply is the problem identified and the solution communicated to any audience?
▲
Presentation
Visual design quality, delivery confidence, and the ability to captivate an audience.
◈
Feasibility
Is the solution practical, grounded, and achievable in the real world?
🌐
SDG Alignment
Meaningful contribution toward the UN Sustainable Development Goals.
